Possible Solutions

Affordable Housing in Suburbs

Increasing the availability of affordable housing in better neighborhoods gives families access to better services.

Equally Distribute School Funding

Instead of schools being funded by local property taxes, evenly divide all state property taxes to schools.

Monitor Real-Estate Industry

Pass laws to better regulate the real-estate industry and prevent any racial bias in the process.

Introduce Mobility Grants

Assist people moving to neighborhoods where there presence would help integration.

Increase Housing Voucher Funding

Increase funding to decrease the waitlist to receive housing vouchers.

Increase Government Assistance

Create programs like the historic Model Cities program to improve the segregated parts of cities.
